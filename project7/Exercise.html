Excercise Overview

To be active and moving is an important thing in our deaily lives. It could be as minimal as going out for a walk. The reason people should incorporate excercises to their routine could be the many benefits from stamina, strength, and health.

When performing an excercises below it's important to know that you may not reach the amount of reps listed, but try to do as many as you can! Something is better than nothing!!

Progressive Overload

As you repeat some of the exercises you should level up your exercise and do more than what you can previously do each time you. This type of training involves choosing a goal and working to challenge yourself and slowly increase the intensity of the workouts each time. This could be done by either adding more weight, more repetitions or decreasing rest periods each time.
